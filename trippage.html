Itinerary

Day 1:
Kickstart Your Dubai Experience In Style! The Day Will Be All About Cruising And Enjoying The Arabian Nights.
  • Arrive at Dubai airport and transfer to the hotel.
  • Check in to the hotel and rest for a while.
  • In the evening enjoy the Dhow cruise dining experience on Marina. Experience this cruise dinner while cruising      through the waters of Dubai and witness Dubai's shining horizon with a large range of &dishes offering flavors      from everywhere in the world. Enjoy Tanoura Show & Belly Dance.
  • After having dinner and some splendid time head back to the hotel.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel in Dubai.
    • Day 2:
    Experience Both Traditional And Modern Dubai In A Day. Keep Your Energy Intact For The Thrilling Dune Bashing In The Evening.
  • Post breakfast depart for Dubai City Tour.
  • Places to visit during the Dubai city tour: Panoramic view of Jumeirah Mosque, Burj Al Arab Hotel View, the Palm,      Atlantis Hotel, Jumeirah Beach and Sheik Zayed Road.
  • Around 3:30 pm depart for Desert Safari. Desert Safari in Dubai is a once-in-a-lifetime event that will give you an adrenaline rush as soon as you hit the sands in a vehicle with full torque and power. Enjoy Dune bashing.
  • After the safari enjoys activities like shisha, Belly dance show, Tanura show, and Fire show followed by BBQ dinner.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel in Dubai.
    • Day 3:
    Admire The City Scrapers From Another Yet Remarkable Skyscraper. Take A Tour Of Dubai Mall, Which Is A Lot More Than Just A Shopping Centre. Overnight Stay In Dubai.
  • Post breakfast, get ready to tour the tallest building in the world. From the top of the 124th floor of Burj Khalifa, get a glimpse into the city beneath and beyond!
  • Explore Dubai Mall, a Mall like no other.
  • Explore the rest of the day on your own.
  • Head back to the hotel.
  • Overnight stay in the hotel.
    • Day 4:
    Time To Explore The Capital City Of The Emirates. Ride The Speed Here.
  • Post breakfast, get ready for the Abu Dhabi city tour by 8 am.
  • Places to visit during the city tour: Sheik Zayed Mosque etc.
  • Visit Ferrari World with a One Day One Park ticket (park timings 11 am to 8 pm). Enjoy all the activities.
  • Return to Dubai at 5 pm.
  • Enjoy the fountain show underneath the Burj Khalifa. This 30-minute show is an unmissable attraction in Dubai.
  • Dinner and overnight stay in the hotel.
    • Day 5:
    Leisure Day | DIY
  • Post breakfast you are free to Explore Dubai on your own.
  • You are free to explore the city on your own or get indulged in any adventure activity.
  • The cost of various adventure activities is mentioned in the Notes section and will be an add-on cost to the trip.
  • Later come back to the hotel. Overnight stay at the hotel in Dubai.
    • Day 6:
    Check Out From The Hotel. Peek Into How Your Future Looks At Dubai Future Museum. Fly Back Home.
  • Post breakfast, check -out and depart for the Dubai Future Museum tour.
  • Collect your baggage and depart for your return flight according to the flight timings.

    • NOTE: Optional Add-On: Full day Jebel Jaish Tour @ ₹12,000/person (Includes pickup and drop from the hotel + ZIP Line + Sledge)- min 6 people required.

    Highlight

  • The tallest building, fastest roller-coaster, and a peek into the future, DUBAI bears it all. Every traveller wishes to witness the transformation from a quaint fishing village surrounded by vast desert planes to a thriving cultural melting pot.
  • Popular for its extravagant shopping centres, ultimate architecture, and astonishing vehicles. The food, the Emirati culture, individuals, the city's back streets and mosques, and the heavenly sites all over the city make it one of a kind. We'll have the option to see all sides of the city with a city tour, including the lavish and the cultural! We will visit the Burj Khalifa, spend an evening in the desert drenched in the culture, and wonder about the greatness of the Persian Gulf and its islands from the Jumeriah beach and the Palm's artificial island!
  • The architectural wonders of mosques and hotels are also included! The evening desert entertainment and Dubai's fountain show bring the event to a close, leaving us with a bag full of memories.
  • If your adrenaline rush has still not touched its peak, you now have an option to explore the adventurous emirate of Ras Al Khaimah. A picturesque city located in the Al Hajar mountain range's foothills. The city screams adventure, and you should be eager to take it on! It's a favourite destination for adrenaline seekers, with tours of the world's highest mountain peak, Jebel Jais, and the thrill of riding the world's longest zip line! The vistas in this area are untouched and unparalleled, while farms and communities can be found in the mountains.
  • Do you want to immerse yourself in Emirati culture's luxury? Join us on a journey in Dubai.
